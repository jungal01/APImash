Sometimes it's necessary to tell someone to fuck off.

And sometimes, that person might not speak English.

That shouldn't stop you though, which is why you have come here. Regardless of the language they may speak, you can express how you really feel with this handy API.

Instructions: in the action field, enter an action this piece of trash should be doing. In the name field, place the name of this "person". In the from field, place the name of person filled with righteous anger.
Not every field will get used every time the content is created, but the from must be filled, and it's useful to fill them all, as the content is randomly generated. Once the fields are filled out to your pleasure, hit the generate button to create the content. Then select the language you would like to translate to and hit translate button.


Important info: the server likes to hang, so in the event that the English doesn't generate, hit ctrl+f5. Not all languages have been assigned their proper name, some are still in their ISO 639-1 codes, as the associative array is huge (nearly all of the ones on google translate!) and one dev can only do so much. However, that will limit the choices of languages down to the options that have their proper name.
